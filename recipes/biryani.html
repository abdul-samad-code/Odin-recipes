ChikenBiryani


description

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish made with aromatic spices, tender chicken, and basmati rice. It is a perfect combination of taste and fragrance.

This dish is popular during special occasions and is loved for its rich texture and delicious layers of rice and masala.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • Biryani masala
  • Garlic & ginger paste
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Cook onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken, spices, and cook well.
  4. Add rice and water, then cook on low flame.
  5. Let it steam for 10 minutes and serve hot.
